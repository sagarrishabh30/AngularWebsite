This venture was initiated in 2018 by an experienced food and beverage entrepreneur who boasts over 25 years of industry expertise. The primary goal was to cater to consumers seeking wholesome and untainted products. Recognizing the business's potential, two additional individuals joined as co-owners in 2022.

Presently, our manufacturing facility has the capability to produce approximately 120 kg of candies daily, equivalent to 3,600 kg per month or 43,000 kg per year. To meet the increasing consumer demand, we are currently expanding our facility. Once the expansion is complete, our production capacity will soar to nearly 4,000 kg of candies daily, translating to 12,000 kg per month or an impressive 140,000 kg per year.

Furthermore, we are dedicated to introducing an array of new offerings to captivate and satisfy our valued consumers.
